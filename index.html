The Hollywood Reporter describes it as "China's first full-scale interstellar spectacular". The Wandering Earth (Chinese: 流浪地球) is a Chinese science fiction film directed by Frant Gwo. It is based on the novella of the same name by the Locus Award and Hugo Award winning author Liu Cixin, and was released on February 5, 2019 (the 2019 Chinese New Year's Day).The Hollywood Reporter describes it as "China's first full-scale interstellar spectacular".

Basic Information

  • Genres: Sci-Fi
  • Runtime: 125 min
  • Director: Frant Gwo
  • Writers: Gong Geer, Junce Ye etc.
  • Stars: Guangjie Li, Chuxiao Qu, Man-Tat Ng

Storyline

The sun is dying out. The earth will soon be engulfed by the inflating sun. To save the human civilization, scientists draw up an escape plan that will bring the whole human race from danger. With the help of thousands of infusion powered engines, the planet earth will leave the solar system and embark on a 2,500 year journey to the orbit of a star 4.5 light years away.
